Hi, my name is Jenn B & I have some advice for you!

Take a Pause

No one said this was going to be easy. When it's getting frustrating & you feel like giving up, take pause. Walk around the block. Distract yourself with something and come back to the problem with a clear head.

Don't be Afraid to Ask for Help

Read the slack channel for informative information you may come across. Ask for help and don't feel ashamed. Everyone has gone through your frustration and is willing to help you. Reach out if all else fails.

Never Give Up

Sometimes it is just not clicking, but don't give up. Take a break, ask for help, but never give up. If life wasn't challenging, no one would ever make the effort to be great. You're on the right path, you just have to trust it.
