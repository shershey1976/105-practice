Hi, my name is Mihaela and I have some advice for you!

If it scares you, it means you're on the right path!

At first, when you start learning how to code everything looks impossible. You may have a unique vision for your future projects and tons of ideas, yet no clue how to bring them to life!

It's exciting, isn't it? You're in front of a blank page and so many possibilities await! Don't get discouraged, though. If you feel like running away, it means you're on the right track. Keep going, step by step.

You'll start small and build things, pass important programming milestones and learn a lot of new concepts.If you feel you're not good enough, let me tell you a secret. Most of programmers don't, yet they still do it and finish the projects. The important thing is to keep going.

Everyone is here to help and cheer you on your way!

Tech community is one of the friendliest possible. Let me tell you another secret: everyone wants to help! So give people the chance to help you grow.

So don't be afraid to ask questions. It is actually better if you do, because you'll see accelerated growth in your programming journey. You are not alone!

Congrats on starting Your Tech Journey. Rise and shine, Skillcrusher!
