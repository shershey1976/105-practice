Hi, my name is Tefi and I have some advice for you!

Don't limit yourslef!

Let go of all of the negative ideas in your mind that tell you, "you can't do this". That is your brain protecting you from "harm".
You see, when you learn something new, your brain protects you from the unknown. There is surely a learning curve (as with anything in life) so don't give up thinking you can't do it, because you can!
The first step is to give it a try!

Give yourself some grace

You are learning something new and you can't expect to know it all from the start. You also shouldn't compare to others.
Watch/read the lessons, practice, do your homework and eventually you'll get better.
Don't foget to take breaks, our brain works better when we spend time of deep learning followed by breaks so that it can "absorbe" the information and create new pathways.
